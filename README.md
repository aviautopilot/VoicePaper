## 🎧 How to Use VoicePaper

VoicePaper combines several learning methods. Here is a simple guide to its features.

---

### Bilingual Reading

VoicePaper shows English text paired with its Chinese translation. The app plays the English audio synced with the highlighted sentence or word. This helps understanding and pronunciation.

- Use play, pause, and skip controls to follow the audio.
- Adjust audio speed in settings if needed.
- Click on words to see definitions and add them to your review list.

---

### Vocabulary Review with Spaced Repetition

VoicePaper uses the SM-2 algorithm to schedule review sessions for words you want to remember.

- Add new words from readings manually or automatically.
- Review vocabulary at spaced intervals prompted by the app.
- Track your progress with simple graphs and stats.

---

### Dictation Practice

In dictation mode, VoicePaper reads sentences or passages aloud. You type what you hear into the app.

- The app scores your typing for accuracy.
- Correct and incorrect parts are highlighted.
- Retry exercises or move on as your skills improve.
- Dictation helps with listening, spelling, and writing.

---

### Gamification Features

To keep you motivated, VoicePaper includes small game elements like:

- Points for completing exercises
- Badges for milestones
- Progress bars for goals

These features aim to encourage consistent practice without distraction.
